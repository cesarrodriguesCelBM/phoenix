Who we are

The Phoenix Hidrogênio emerged from the need for a new perspective for society, that integrally looks at the planet. This novel perspective is focused on promoting today what we want as a future.

With the strategy of being first Brazilian technology company in the use of hydrogen in scale .As the first Brazilian technology company to fully rely on the use of hydrogen on large scale, our practices are based on sustainability and eco- efficient processes. Our goal is based not only on what society expects from a company, but also on what meets their real needs.

Our beginning

In 2005, based on an old dream transforming the way energy resources are used, the founder of Phoenix Hidrogênio, Jorge Israel, begins his trajectory. In his garage, he looks for a way to save fuel using an alternative source. He begins the saga of efficiency, reliability, and continuous improvement of the hydrogen production process. After countless attempts, he develops a working prototype that had been tested in several conditions and with extremely satisfactory results. This outcome proves the efficiency of the system. In 2021, the initiative takes shape with the implementation of the project in scalar form. By bringing together a multidisciplinary team of technicians, and initiating the implementation of the UDT - Technology Development Unit, PHOENIX HIDROGENIO is on its way to conquering markets.
