Who we are

The Phoenix Hidrogênio It emerged from the incessant search for a new consciousness of society, which looks at the planet in an integral way, promoting today, what we want as a future.

With the prospect of being first Brazilian technology company in the use of hydrogen in scale . From the development of programs and projects, based on sustainability and eco-efficiency practices providing solutions that meet society's expectations.

Historical

In 2005, based on an old dream, that of carrying out a transformation in the way of using energy resources, the founder of Phoenix Hidrogênio, Jorge Israel, begins its trajectory. In its own garage, seeks a way to save fuel using an alternative source.

The saga for efficiency, reliability and continuous improvement of the hydrogen production process, after numerous attempts, Jorge Israel develops a working prototype, which has been tested in several conditions and with extremely satisfactory results. This proves the system's efficiency.

In 2021, the initiative takes shape with the implementation of the project in scalar form. Gathering a multidisciplinary team of technicians, initiating the implementation of the TDU - Technology Development Unit, PHOENIX HIDROGENIO walks to conquer markets.

Institutional Objective

Provide the company with tools and means to insert its technologies, in the world market/scenario, aimed at the production of clean energy. Through research production and patent registration.
